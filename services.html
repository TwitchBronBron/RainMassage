Services

The following services are currently offered:

30 Minute Swedish Massage: $35
A 30 minute Swedish Massage can be either a full body massage or a focused area massage, focusing on the back, arms, legs front and back, neck, shoulders, hands, feet, glutes or abdominal muscles. The focused area massage is designed to release tension in any part of the body that is irritated, tense, tight, or is simply in need of special attention.

60 Minute Swedish Massage: $60
A 60 Minute Swedish Massage generally consists of the face, neck, shoulders, arms, hands, legs front and back, glutes, back, feet, and sometimes a scalp massage depending on preference. Abdominal muscles can be included per request.

90 Minute Swedish Massage: $90
A 90 Minute Swedish Massage is an extended version of the 60 Minute Swedish Massage. The massage includes face, neck, shoulders, arms, hands, legs front and back, glutes, back, feet, and a scalp massage depending on preference. Abdominal Muscles can be included per request. With a 90 Minute Swedish Massage, the muscles endure and experience a longer time period allowing for a deep relaxation or deep therapeutic relief massage.

Special Attention Massage: $1 Per Minute of Massage. (Massages under 40 Minutes will be charged a service fee of $5, unless more than one massage is being given to multiple individuals at one location)
Special Attention Massage is helpful when you are pressed for time and only have 40-45 minutes to spare instead of the full hour. With this massage, focus is given to one arm, one leg, the back, the neck, the hands, the feet, the calves, the thighs, or the whole body. It is entirely up to you.

*NOTE: Special attention can be paid to any of the muscles listed above in order to provide a better and lengthier therapeutic massage experience as well as providing a release of tensed muscles. Any part of any massage can be omitted per request.

60 Minute Prenatal/Postpartum Massage: $63
An hour long prenatal massage is great for moms to be! It's an hour of relaxation, stretching, and pure bliss. Side lying positions are used during this service as well as multiple pillows for comfort. We also offer Postpartum massages are also offered upon request.

60 or 90 Minute Hot and/or Cold Stone Massage: $65 / $95

A hot and/or cold stone massage can be administered in 60 minutes or 90 minutes. The heat from the stones soothes sore achy muscles and helps to reduce stress. Hot stone massage can help to lessen chronic pain, reduce muscle stiffness, and expand blood vessels which increases circulation! It's the gold standard of relaxation with these water heated stones! The cold stone massage constricts the blood vessels in the body to help alleviate pain and swelling. The cold stones are fantastic on trigger points without being invasive to the muscles. The cold stones are also great for cooling off and can be just as relaxing as the hot stone massage! Cold stones are great for clients that prefer cold over heat. Both hot and cold stones promote healing in their own way! A contrast massage can also be applied and is incredibly beneficial. You get the benefits of both Hot Stone and Cold Stone in one massage by alternating between the two methods!

Paraffin Wax Treatment: $10 per treatment.
The benefits of Paraffin wax are bountiful. Some of the benefits include helping to relieve achy joints, relax stiff muscles, moisturize dry cracked skin, increase range of motion, smooth rough hands, open pores and increase circulation wherever applied. This treatment is not ideal for oily types of skin. This treatment leaves the skin soft, moisturized, and feeling great. There will be a small price increase if additional wax is needed for a larger surface area (i.e. large area of the legs, entire forearm, back, etc.). Bamboo Massage: $38 / $63 / $93
Bamboo Massage is just as beneficial as a Swedish massage but with longer lasting effects. A variety of smooth bamboo sticks and stones are used in this full body massage. The bamboo sticks allow the therapist to get better leverage when working on tense muscles, helping to deeply relax muscles, and may feel less invasive than certain deep tissue massages. The bamboo sticks can be used to knead and stretch fascia and muscle in all directions, promote deep relaxation, and release trigger points, knots, and tension. Although only light to medium pressue is applied, the resulting effects are similar to those of a deep tissue massage. The bamboo massage sticks can be heated (similarly to hot stone massage) or can be used at room temperature. This massage can be administered in 30, 60, or 90 minute massage sessions.
